This sakura star design is made by Ali Bahmani. It is an advanced origami design, but it is worth the effort because it can be either a star or a flower, depending on where you place it.

Fun fact: Sakura blossoms were donated by Japan to the United States as a sign of piece and friendship. For more than a hundred years, the citizens of Washington D.C. celebrate the blooming of cherry trees every year. The idea to grow them along the Potomac waterfront originated courtesy of Mrs. Eliza Ruhamah Scidmore, a world traveler, writer, and diplomat.
